Welcome to FORRT (Framework for Open and Reproducible Research Training)! This page will help you get started and become an active member of our community.

## Organisational Structure & Projects

### How FORRT is Organised

FORRT operates as a community-driven initiative with various teams working on different aspects:

- **Education Team** - Develops teaching materials and pedagogies
- **Research Team** - Works on research projects and publications  
- **Outreach Team** - Handles communications and community engagement
- **Technical Team** - Maintains the website and digital infrastructure

### Current Projects

Explore our active projects and find ways to contribute:

1. **[FORRT Clusters](/clusters)** - Our 7-cluster framework for open science education
2. **[Educational Resources](/resources)** - Curated materials for teaching open science
3. **[Glossary](/glossary)** - Comprehensive glossary of open science terms
4. **[Summaries](/summaries)** - Accessible summaries of key research papers

## Paper-Writing Guidelines

Interested in contributing to FORRT publications? Here are the key guidelines:

### Getting Involved

1. **Express Interest** - Reach out on Slack or via email
2. **Review Guidelines** - Familiarize yourself with our collaborative writing process
3. **Join a Writing Team** - Connect with existing project leads

### Writing Standards

- Use inclusive and accessible language
- Follow open science principles
- Properly cite all sources
- Collaborate openly with co-authors

### Resources

- Check the [Publications](/publications) page for past work
- Review our [Manuscript Guidelines](/manuscript) for detailed instructions
