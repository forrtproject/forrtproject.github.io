Motivation

To provide educators with an example of how they can draw resources from FORRT‚Äôs educational nexus to integrate open scholarship into their teaching, FORRT has developed an Open and Reproducible Science 101 syllabus.

FORRT‚Äôs syllabus was developed as a seminar series covering 9 weeks of teaching. As such, the proposed topic for each week is one of FORRT‚Äôs clusters and its related literature. For each week, there are suggestions of core and additional readings. The syllabus is comprised of a series of assignments and proposed activities for students.

Importantly, this syllabus is not intended to be a ‚Äúone size fits all‚Äù approach. While educators are welcomed to use this syllabus in its current format, they can also adapt it to meet their current courses and needs. We hope it can serve as starting point for your class.
